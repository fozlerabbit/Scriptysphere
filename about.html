About ScriptySphere

Learn about our mission, values, and the passionate team driving global technology innovation forward.

Our Mission

At ScriptySphere, we believe technology has the power to transform lives and create a more equitable world. Our mission is to empower communities worldwide through innovative technology solutions, digital literacy programs, and sustainable initiatives that create lasting positive impact.

Since our founding in 2010, we've worked tirelessly to bridge the digital divide and ensure that everyone, regardless of their background or location, has access to the tools and knowledge needed to thrive in the digital age.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our history of innovation and impact

2010

Foundation

ScriptySphere was founded with a vision to democratize technology education and innovation.

2013

First International Program

Launched our first digital literacy program in partnership with organizations across 5 countries.

2016

Million Learners Reached

Celebrated impacting our first million learners through various technology education initiatives.

2020

Pandemic Response

Rapidly scaled our digital programs to support remote learning during global lockdowns.

2024

Global Expansion

Reached 50+ countries with sustainable technology initiatives and digital skills programs.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Inclusion

We believe technology should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background, location, or resources.

Innovation

We constantly seek new approaches and creative solutions to address global challenges through technology.

Sustainability

We develop solutions that are environmentally responsible and create lasting impact for future generations.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical practices in all our initiatives and partnerships.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and work closely with communities to co-create solutions.

Impact
